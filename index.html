Calculation Method

Health % = (Current Capacity ÷ Design Capacity) × 100
Health % = 100 − (Cycles ÷ Max Rated Cycles) × Degradation %
Health % = ((V_current − V_min) ÷ (V_max − V_min)) × 100
Health % = (Actual Runtime ÷ Rated Runtime) × 100

Health Grade Reference

Health % Grade Meaning Action
90 – 100% Excellent Like new, full capacity Continue normal use
75 – 89% Good Minor wear, reliable No action needed
50 – 74% Fair Noticeable degradation Monitor closely
< 50% Poor Significant capacity loss Consider replacement

All Formulas

📐 Capacity: H = (C_now ÷ C_design) × 100
🔄 Cycle: H = 100 − (n ÷ N) × D
🔌 Voltage: H = ((V − V_min) ÷ (V_max − V_min)) × 100
⏱️ Time: H = (T_actual × T_factor ÷ T_rated) × 100
Penalties: age >24mo → −0.3%/mo · temp >30°C → −0.2%/°C above 30
